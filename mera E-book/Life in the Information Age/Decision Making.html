Decision Making

Due to the huge number of information sources available to us, making decision and choosing one over the other is very difficult. There is an information overload in front of us.
Even simple things like buying a smartphone of your choice turns out to be a near impossible task because the user is bombarded with numerous other choices and data. More than choosing what you want, figuring out whether the content is genuine, legit and legal is the more difficult part as of now. Most of the content that is uploaded on to the internet is not validated properly.


Many users therefore, find it really difficult to believe most of the content on the internet. However, there are ICT tools to manage this overload of information.
