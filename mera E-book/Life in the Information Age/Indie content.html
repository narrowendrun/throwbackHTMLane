Entertainment and leisure

Independent content can also be published online now, without the individuals having to wait for other people to grant them permission or being afraid of their content being edited by an external person. A prime example where individuals are allowed to publish their videos is YouTube. Another aspect is the emergence of video games! Video games have become so popular that a whole industry for gaming has been created. It is the leisure activity that most people would like to engage with. With more advanced gaming hardware getting release, like the new PS4 pro, gaming experiences are becoming more immersive. Other such examples are VR headsets like the Oculus Rift or the HTC Vive which allow the users to experience a virtual reality. They can get engaged in a lot of engaging activities like playing games. With the help of ICT nowadays, almost everything in movies can be created digitally, which are sometimes close to real or animated. Few examples of great CGI effect and animations are The Jungle Book, Ex-Machina and Inside Out.
