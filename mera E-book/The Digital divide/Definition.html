What is the digital divide

The digital divide is the technology gap that exists between two groups. These groups can be societies, countries, economic areas or anything else. It is a major issue from technical, social and economic perspectives.
There are countless reasons for such divides of which a few are age, status and location. This divide is experienced by everybody at all levels. The more common gaps can be experienced at the national and international levels, among and within countries.
